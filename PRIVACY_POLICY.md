# Privacy Policy for Hey DJ

**Last Updated: May 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how "Hey DJ" (referred to as "the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Audio Recordings**: When you use the "Record" feature, the app records your mix. These files are stored locally on your device in the `/Music/HeyDJ` folder. We do not upload these recordings to our servers.
- **Custom Samples**: If you record custom samples using the microphone, these are stored locally on your device.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Identifiers**: We collect standard Android advertising IDs and device identifiers to facilitate Google AdMob and Google Play Billing services.
- **Location Information**: We may collect approximate location data through Google AdMob to serve relevant regional advertisements.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App utilizes the following third-party services that may collect data used to identify you:
- **Google Play Services**: For app core functionality.
- **Google AdMob**: For serving advertisements. [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Play Billing**: For managing your monthly subscription. [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 3. How We Use Your Data
- **App Functionality**: To process audio, manage your music library, and save your recorded sets.
- **Monetization**: To show non-intrusive ads to free users and manage the "Premium" subscription status.
- **Analytics**: To monitor app performance and fix technical issues in the native audio engine.

## 4. Permissions
The App requests the following sensitive permissions:
- **RECORD_AUDIO**: Required for capturing live sets and custom samples.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE**: Required to keep the audio engine running while you use other apps or lock your screen.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: To show playback and recording status in the system drawer.
