# Hey DJ — The Professional Mixing Console

Transform your Android device into a high-fidelity DJ workstation. Hey DJ combines the tactile feel of classic hardware with a powerful C++ native audio engine for sub-millisecond latency and professional sound quality.

## Two Ways to Mix
- **Classic Layout**: A spacious, intuitive design perfect for beginners or smaller screens.
- **Pro Layout (Premium)**: A high-density "Command Center" featuring rotary knobs, long-throw vertical EQ faders (100dp travel), and color-coded transport pads for ultimate control.

## Professional Features
- **High-Performance Native Engine**: Real-time stereo summing and synchronized deck mixing.
- **Advanced DSP Effects**: Reverb, Echo, Flanger, Filter, Phaser, and more to shape your sound.
- **Interactive Turntables**: Realistic vinyl rotation with high-precision scratching and manual beat-matching.
- **Spectral Waveforms**: Three-band color-coded waveforms (Bass, Mids, Highs) for visual mixing.
- **Studio Recording**: Capture your live sets in high-quality .m4a format directly to your device's Music folder.
- **Custom Sampler & Drum Pad**: Trigger built-in or custom samples to spice up your performance.
