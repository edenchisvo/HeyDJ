# Data Deletion Policy — Hey DJ

At Hey DJ, we respect your privacy and provide simple ways to manage and delete your data.

## 1. Local Data Deletion
The majority of the data created by the app is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time:
- **Recordings**: Go to your device's `/Music/HeyDJ` folder and delete any `.m4a` files.
- **Track Database**: In Android Settings, go to **Apps > Hey DJ > Storage** and select **"Clear Data."** This will remove all local track indexing and playlists.

## 2. Subscription & ID Data
- **Financial Data**: Since we use Google Play Billing, we do not store your credit card or billing info. To remove this data, you must manage your Google account at [Google Payments](https://pay.google.com).
- **Advertising ID**: You can reset or delete your Android Advertising ID in your phone settings under **Privacy > Ads**.

## 3. Requesting Data Deletion
If you wish to request the deletion of any data that may have been collected via our third-party analytics or support interactions, please email us at edenaxis1@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request." 

We will process all legitimate requests within 30 days.
